# Course Creation

After logging in, instructors can create and manage courses. Each course acts as a central workspace for organizing textbooks, question banks, quizzes, and AI-powered tools.

---

## Creating a Course

To create a new course, you will be prompted to provide the following information:

- **Course Name**
- **Course Term** (e.g., Fall 2026)
- **Course Description**
- **Upload Textbook** (only **one textbook** is allowed per course)
- **Card Color** (used to visually organize and distinguish your courses)

---

## Course Setup Process

1. Navigate to the **Create Course** page  
2. Fill in all required fields  
3. Upload your course textbook  
4. Choose a card color  
5. Submit the form to create your course  

Once created, your course will appear as a **card** on your dashboard.

---

## Editing a Course

If you made a mistake during creation, you can easily update your course:

- Locate your course card on the dashboard  
- Click the **edit option** in the **bottom-right corner** of the card  
- Modify the course details as needed  
- Save your changes  

:::tip
You can update course information at any time to better reflect your curriculum.
:::

---

## Important Notes

- Only **one textbook** can be uploaded per course  
- The selected **card color** helps visually organize multiple courses  
- Editing allows you to quickly fix mistakes without recreating the course  

---

## Summary

- Courses require basic information and a textbook upload  
- Each course is displayed as a customizable card  
- You can edit courses anytime using the bottom-right edit option  

:::danger Important
Make sure your uploaded textbook is correct, as it will be used to generate question banks and power the AI Tutor.
:::
