# AI Tutor Creation

The **AI Tutor** is a course-aware chatbot powered by the textbook you uploaded. It allows instructors to create a customized teaching assistant that interacts with students based on course content and instructor-defined behavior.

---
![AI TutorPage](/img/ai-chatbot.png)

## Creating an AI Tutor

To set up your AI Tutor, you will configure the following:

- **Tutor Name**
- **Persona Preset** (e.g., Socratic Tutor, Study Buddy, Quiz Master)
- **Welcome Message**
- **System Prompt** (defines how the AI behaves)

---

## Configuring Behavior

The **system prompt** allows you to control how the AI Tutor responds. You can define:

- **Teaching style** (e.g., guiding vs. direct answers)  
- **Topic focus** (limit responses to specific subjects or chapters)  
- **Restrictions** (prevent answering unrelated or off-topic questions)  

:::tip
A well-written system prompt helps ensure the AI Tutor stays aligned with your course goals and teaching style.
:::

---

## Using Textbook Context

- The AI Tutor is grounded in the **textbook you uploaded**  
- When a student asks a question, relevant sections are retrieved and used to generate responses  
- This ensures answers are **accurate and course-specific**  

---

## Previewing the AI Tutor

After configuring your tutor:

- You can **preview** the chatbot  
- Test how it responds to questions  
- Adjust the system prompt, persona, or welcome message as needed  

---

## Activating the AI Tutor

Once you are satisfied with your configuration:

1. Click **Save** to store your AI Tutor  
2. Click **Activate** to make it available for student use  

---

## Summary

- AI Tutors are customizable, course-aware chatbots  
- You control behavior using personas and system prompts  
- Previewing allows you to test before activation  
- Once activated, students can interact with the tutor  

:::danger Important
Make sure your system prompt and restrictions are clearly defined to prevent inaccurate or unintended responses.
:::
